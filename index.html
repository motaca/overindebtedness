OVER INDEBTEDNESS

the idea

It is about an initiative to know who and how many are the over indebted. Respond in a humane and non-stigmatized way because this situation is increasingly common in developing countries. Propose solutions to combat the marginalization of citizens and address the issue in a sensitive, healthy and constructive way.

who am I?

I'm Flávia, an engineer, data scientist and over indebted (under treatment). I grew up in a house where you never talked about money, not even when the collectors knocked on our door, but I believe I can get out of this situation and get back to it in no time. I believe in a world where information and generosity are available to all.

why speak of over indebtedness?

First, because I believe that talking about problems is a tool for transforming the individual and society. When I realized this, I noticed that people do not talk about it. And mainly: there is no data, studies or clinical research that supports the subject. The super indebted are excluded from the financial market and most of the time this damage is also familiar and social.
